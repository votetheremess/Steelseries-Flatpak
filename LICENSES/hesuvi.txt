HeSuVi HRIR Files
=================

Files:
  data/hrtf/gsx.wav       (from HeSuVi's hrir/gsx.wav)
  data/hrtf/dh2.wav       (from HeSuVi's hrir/dh++.wav)
  data/hrtf/dts-hpx.wav   (from HeSuVi's hrir/dtshx.wav)

All three are 14-channel 48 kHz IEEE-float WAV impulse-response files
extracted from HeSuVi 2.0.0.1 (SourceForge installer, 27 MB).

Source project:
  HeSuVi — Headphone Surround Virtualization
  Author: jaakkopasanen (Jaakko Pasanen)
  Homepage: https://sourceforge.net/projects/hesuvi/
